Biographies

Band Members

Harvey Dale: Harvey is the lead singer and bassist. He was born in 1994 in Ontario, Canada. From an early age Harvey expressed a passion for music.

Jackie Watterson: Lead guitarist of the band, Jackie was born in 1987 in Columbus, Ohio. He learned to play guitar from a local tutor named Ibuki Mioda.

Dave Martins: Dave is the band's drummer. Dave was born in 1991 in Chattanooga, Tennesee. From pots and pans to car hoods and eventually actual drums, Dave has always loved to rock out.

Jazmine Gutierrez: Jazmine is the band's one and only keyboardist. She was born in 1979 in Cancun, Mexico. She's the band's oldest member, but that doesn't mean she doesn't know how to party, or rock out for that matter.
